How It Works

Screening

Potential investors are rigorously screened to ensure that entrepreneurs have the best investors at their fingertips.

Catalog

Startups are cataloged through user-generated tags guaranteeing the highest visibility with investors who specialize in a certain field.

Security

Entreprenuers have the option to encrypt or restrict access to certain documents. This allows entreprenuers to maintain the confidentiality of their intellectual property.

Facilitation

InnoVC facilitates interaction between entrepreneurs and investors, allowing for seamless transaction regardless of geographical barriers.

About Us

InnoVC is an online marketplace that aims to globalize startup investment. We provide a platform where entrepreneurs can securely upload and share their ideas. Corporate investors, who pass a mandatory screening, can view uploaded ideas and decide in which ideas they would like to invest. This generates a world of possibilities for innovators who may not have readily available access to venture capitalists. Ideally, someone can receive an investment from halfway around the world.
